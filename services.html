Apple Care+

Apple Care+ for 1 year | Price: £219

AppleCare+ is an extended warranty and support program offered by Apple for their devices. It provides additional coverage and protection beyond the standard warranty. With AppleCare+, you receive priority access to Apple's customer support, including technical assistance and troubleshooting. It also offers coverage for accidental damage, such as drops or spills, subject to a deductible fee. AppleCare+ provides peace of mind by ensuring that your Apple device is protected and that you can receive timely support and repairs if needed.

Screen Protection

Applying Screen Protection for any phone | Price: £15

Applying a screen protector offers additional protection for your device's display. Typically made of tempered glass or film, screen protectors are designed to guard against scratches, smudges, and fingerprints. They feature a precise fit, ensuring full coverage and compatibility with touchscreens. The application process involves cleaning the screen, aligning the protector, and smoothing out any air bubbles. Once applied, a screen protector maintains screen clarity and touch sensitivity while adding a layer of defense against daily wear and tear.
